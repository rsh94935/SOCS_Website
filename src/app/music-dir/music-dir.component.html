Music Director

George Salmon

I am looking forward to working on a range of different music with the Sittingbourne Orpheus Coral Society. When I construct a programme I like to include a varied repertoire, ranging from choral society staples to lesser known works by the great masters, as well as lighter material – always with an eye to the specific challenges each piece raises, how these will improve the choir and, above all, what people will enjoy singing and hearing!

My work spans a number of genres from choral and operatic conducting to brass banding and playing in string quartets. I have worked on opera projects with the Amade Players and Goldsmiths Opera Chorus, as well as numerous appearances with various orchestras in and around London.

I have had the privilege of studying with some of the most acclaimed conducting tutors in the world, including Peter Stark, Colin Metters and Jorma Panula, I also hold a Masters Degree in conducting from the University of Surrey. I am an accomplished singer, having studied with Alison Wells – Professor of Singing at the Royal College of Music and Trinity Laban Conservatoire.
